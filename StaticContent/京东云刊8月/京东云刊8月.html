Issuing of defense-in-depth scheme of enterprise application

Under the complex network environment, business is faced with various safety threats, such as DDos attach, CC attach, hacker intrusion, library hitting, website distortion, malicious crawling and website malicious code. All those influence normal operation of business and impair enterprise brand image. Based on multiple years of accumulations for providing safety guarantee for JD Mall, JD Cloud establishes comprehensive safety protection system for your enterprise, so as to ensure core data safety and business continuity and stability.

Launch of application security gateway!

Application safety gateway is the safety product for visualized safety analysis and threat prevention of application layer launched oriented to customer site or APP service. By improving such functions as WAF, user access audit and business safety visual and compliance check, the steady and sustainable operation of business is ensured, the user experience is improved and the abnormality or compliance problem resulted from TTP/HTTPS business being attacked is solved.
